AboutMe

Frontend Developer

Hello! I'm Prajakta Mali, a passionate frontend developer with a keen eye for UI/UX design and a knack for creating responsive, user-friendly interfaces. With 5 years of experience in web development, I've worked extensively with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and popular frontend frameworks like React and Vue.js. My journey into web development started with a fascination for how websites blend creativity with functionality. Over the years, I've honed my skills in translating design mockups into seamless, interactive experiences. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can contribute ideas, learn from others, and iterate quickly to deliver exceptional results. Outside of coding, I enjoy staying updated with the latest trends in frontend development and exploring new tools that enhance efficiency and performance. I'm also passionate about accessibility and strive to create web experiences that are inclusive and accessible to all users.

UI//UX Designing

While UI and UX design are distinct disciplines, they are closely interconnected and often overlap in practice. Successful digital products require collaboration between UI and UX designers to ensure that the interface is both visually appealing (UI) and user-friendly (UX). Effective communication, iterative design processes, and a focus on user needs and feedback are key to creating compelling and functional digital experiences.
